Session Orientation

The time booked is for hands-on massage. Please plan an additional 15-35 minutes for check-in, transitions, and integration/departure time.

Arrival + access

Sessions are hosted in my home studio near Liberty Park. There is a gravel parking spot on the east side of the house (access from the alley), or street parking is available. Please come to the back door and knock.

There is a flight of stairs to the studio. If you have accessibility needs, please reach out beforehand so we can make a plan together.

Beginning the session

We begin with a spacious check-in: how you are, what support you want, and what would make the session feel grounding and nourishing.

Payment is handled before bodywork begins so your departure can stay relaxed and integrated. Tips are welcome and not required.

During and after bodywork

Depending on style, you may undress to your comfort level and be draped with a sheet. Some sessions (such as shiatsu or visceral work) may remain fully clothed.

At the end, there is time for questions and space for anything that is present for you. You might consider a slow walk, extra water, and wholesome food afterward.

What to bring

  • Water to hydrate before and after your session
  • Loose, comfy clothing
  • Cash if possible (Venmo, Zelle, and check also available)

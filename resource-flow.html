Resource Flow + Financial Transparency

This practice is rooted in transparency and discernment. Rather than a traditional for-profit model, sessions are part of a needs-based resource flow experiment.

Session costs

Session length Rate
75 minutes hands-on $125
90 minutes hands-on $150

How this model works

The listed rates reflect what is deeply supportive for sustaining life and practice. Lower-cost and trade sessions are available in limited numbers based on current capacity.

If a smaller group of clients can pay full rate (or above), it helps keep the practice going and widens access for others. Trade for practical support (food, maintenance, and repair skills) can also be discussed.

This is currently a one-year experiment, with quarterly check-ins with a support team to assess what is working and what needs to change.

Estimated needs

Annual baseline needs are estimated at approximately $24,410 (about $2,034/month), covering housing-related needs, food, medical/body care, transportation, retreat practice/training, and massage business expenses.

A fuller, line-item transparency document is available and can be shared with clients who want complete detail.

About Lake Effect Bodywork

Lake Effect Bodywork is a relational massage practice rooted in slowing down, listening, and co-creating care that supports body, heart, and spirit.

Practice orientation

This practice is designed as an experiment in resource flow, transparency, and mutual support. Session exchange is meant to sustain this work while also supporting community access and wider organizing commitments.

The approach centers consent, nervous system care, spacious check-in, and integration. Each session is adapted to what is most supportive for you that day.

Values

  • Care over urgency
  • Consent and communication throughout
  • Financial transparency and discernment
  • Support for queer/trans and local organizing community
  • Connection to place and Great Salt Lake watershed

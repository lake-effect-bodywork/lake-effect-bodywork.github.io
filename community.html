Community Organizing + Cultural Work

Your session helps support unpaid organizing that gathers community, builds relationship with place, and contributes to Great Salt Lake movement work.

Lakewords Collaborative

4+ years of writing and organizing, with over 1,000 handmade books distributed about Great Salt Lake, plus events and collaborative art projects.

Queer Poetry Night

2+ years of co-hosting and administrative labor for a free monthly queer/trans open mic space, including grant writing for ongoing ASL interpretation.

Lake Time

1+ year co-organizing gatherings for deep listening, place relationship, art, food, and emergent community practice at and around the lakeshore.

Great Salt Lake Vigil

4+ years of co-organizing/co-facilitating lake-facing rituals and participatory art, including regular practices during legislative session.

This work is about creating beautiful, irresistible movement culture rooted in care and relationship with the lake, watershed, and more-than-human world.
